## Strategic Recommendation

Build **Dify Quality Hub**: a production-quality layer for Dify that helps teams evaluate, trace, compare, and optimize AI workflows after launch.

This matters because AI builders increasingly need to prove that an app is:

- Accurate enough to trust.
- Debuggable when it fails.
- Affordable to run.
- Safe enough for enterprise review.
- Improving across versions rather than silently regressing.
